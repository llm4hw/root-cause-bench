**Why It's Occurring:**
1. **Port Size Mismatch:**  
   `input1` is declared as 3 bits (indices 2â†’0) while `output` is declared as 4 bits (indices 3â†’0). 
2. **Direct Assignment:**

3. **Synthesis Enforcement:**

**The Fix:**
